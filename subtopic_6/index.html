Metro Manila Traffic:

Why it's so bad

Traffic in Popular Culture and Media

In many films and novels revolving the cities in Metro Manila, traffic usually serves as an unforunate part of everyday life. The commute is hellish and the effort to go from place to place is exhausting. The way a character in a piece of media commutes speaks volumes of their character and socioeconomic statues. This aversion to commuting via the LRTs or MRTs is heavily incorporated into our culture, due to the poor status of our public transport.

In recent events, some incidents of homicide are due to road rage. Shootings and hit-and-runs are caused by traffic incidents. This, along with traffic incidents happen because of traffic congestion, unfortunately costing lives.

This widespread common problem — this gripe that the common people face — has turned deadly in recent years.
